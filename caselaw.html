CASE LAW

Jurisdiction in Stutzland arises from federal and regional legislation, and their interpretation in courts. Since law is not always very direct or simply understood, the Federal Court may have decreed rulings from specific court cases. These rulings form the case law of Stutzland.

Concerning the order of interpretation of different sources of jurisdiction, following applies:

  1. Verfassung (constitution)
  2. Federal laws
  3. Regional laws
  4. Government directives
  5. Case law

As can be seen, case law is not very important and its use is rare. However, certain specific situations may require its implementation.
