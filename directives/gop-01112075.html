    GoP 01/11/2075

    Issuer: Ministry of Finances

    Date of Direction: 01/11/2075


    Contents

    Legal foundation

    1 § - Tax Law

    On the basis of Tax Law (07/11/2033), shall this directive be decreed and applied to the execution of federal legislation and administration.

    Personal taxation

    2 § - Income classes

    There shall be five income classes in federal personal taxation. The classes shall be divided followingly:

    1. up to 32 119 € annual income
    2. up to 39 827 € annual income
    3. up to 57 814 € annual income
    4. up to 77 085 € annual income
    5. up to 102 780 € annual income

    If a taxable subject earns money in between classes, the portion going over the lower income class treshold shall be taxed in accordance to the higher income class percentage.

    3 § - First income class

    First income class shall be taxed 10,00 per cent.

    4 § - Second income class

    Second income class shall be taxed 13,40 per cent.

    5 § - Third income class

    Third income class shall be taxed 17,96 per cent.

    6 § - Fourth income class

    Fourth income class shall be taxed 24,06 per cent.

    7 § - Fifth income class

    Fifth income class shall be taxed 32,24 per cent.
