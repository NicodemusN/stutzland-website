    Federal Insurance Law

    Date of Legislation: 04/04/2055

    Votes to Approve: 23 - 18

    Date of Enforcement: 01/10/2055


    Contents

    1 § - Constitution

    This law, federal insurance law, dictates that which insurances are the citizens of Frankland entitled to, provided by the Federation. In accordance to Verfassung ยง 15 and ยง 16, citizens of Frankland are to be provided with retirement pension and sufficient healthcare. These shall be insured through federal insurances, paid for by natural persons working in Frankland.

    2 § - Federal Healthcare Insurance

    Immediate and emergency care, basic medicinal needs, and wages for emergency room doctors and nurses shall be paid for in part with the Federal Healthcare Insurance. Funding of these services shall not rely solely in the insurance.

    3 § - Healthcare insurance payments

    All working people in Frankland are subject to healthcare insurance payments. These payments shall be collected monthly from wages and can be treated as same as taxes.

    Healthcare insurance payment for each subject shall be 1,05 per cent of monthly gross wage, before taxes have been deducted.

    4 § - Federal Pension Insurance

    The Federation provides retirement pensions for all citizens of Stutzland living in Stutzland past the legal working age. Pension shall be provided in accordance to Pension Law. Funding for pensions may be collected from public and private sources. Federal Pension Insurance shall cover only public share of pensions that are to be paid.

    5 § - Pension insurance payments

    All working people in Frankland are subject to pension insurance payments. These payments shall be collected monthly from wages and can be treated as same as taxes.

    Pension insurance payment for each subject shall be 1,05 per cent of monthly gross wage, before taxes have been deducted.
